Standard Deviation in outcome measure

If you have prior knowledge from a pilot study or the literature, please use that information here. We are asking about the subject-to-subject variability in the units of your outcome measure. A standard deviation from a pilot or literature study may be used.

Our sample size and power calculations assume that the standard deviation does not depend on treatment settings or biological factors. This assumption is called homoscedasticity. For more on standard deviation, please check out our help pages.
