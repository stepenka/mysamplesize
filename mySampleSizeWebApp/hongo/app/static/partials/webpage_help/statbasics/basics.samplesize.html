sample size

Sample Size is the total number of subjects that will be used in an experiment.

6 control subjects 6 treatment subjects
Total Sample Size = 12

Appropriate Sample Size is crucial to reliable, ethical, and efficient experimental results.

  • Detecting a true positive result requires enough samples to average out the variation in test subjects
  • With a small sample, only very large experimental effects can be reliably detected
  • Too large of a sample size can be a waste of resources or -- more importantly -- can lead to the ethical problem of unnecessary use of subjects.
