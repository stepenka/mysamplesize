The Supplemental Report contains forms of the ARRIVE Guidelines. The ARRIVE (Animal Research: Reporting of In Vivo Experiments) Guidelines were developed in an effort to make reporting data thorough, transparent and reproducible. Our supplement tool is an easy to use guide that helps ensure you have documented all of the necessary details of your experiment. The resulting file can be used as a supplement for journal articles or documentation for your reference.

Developed by the National Centre for the Replacement, Refinement and Reductions of Animals in Research (NC3Rs), the ARRIVE Guidelines were created as a tool to standardize and facilitate the communication within the scientific community to improve statistical and reporting methods of research involving live subjects.
