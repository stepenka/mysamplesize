Nested

A Nested design is one in variation of a Factorial design that has hierarchy where one or more factors has sub levels.

This design is commonly used to make a sub level from a living subject that cannot be duplicated or replicated. The individual is often a random effect, meaning that the experiment is not necessarily interesting the effect of the individual on the response but individual must be accounted for to reduce variation within the results.

example

A researcher is interested in the expression of a drug on a cell. The drug is given to 4 subjects and 2 cells will be tested within each individual so that cell is nested within the subject;

statistical tests

Statistical tests that are commonly appropriate for this design:

  • Nested ANOVA
  • Generalized Linear Regression
