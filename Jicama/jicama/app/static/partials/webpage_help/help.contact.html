Professor Ben Fitzpatrick is an applied mathematician and statistician with over 40 years of experience collaborating with engineers, biologists, public health scientists, social scientists, business analysts, and policy makers on quantitative modeling, simulation, and data analysis. He holds the Clarence J. Wallen, S.J., endowed chair in the Mathematics Department at Loyola Marymount University and is President and Principal Analyst of Tempest Technologies.
